## What Tailor Is

A conceptual digital platform for Turkey's small textile producers — addresses the operational inefficiencies and communication barriers in a relationship-driven, SME-heavy industry. Proposes streamlined order management and communication, reducing reliance on costly methods like phone calls.

Core proposed features: real-time messaging, order tracking, demand aggregation (to help producers meet minimum quantity requirements), WhatsApp integration, and an intuitive interface designed for non-technical users.

## Defne's Role (Solo)

- **Needfinding** — conducted interviews with small fashion brands, suppliers, and a textile export VP
- **Competitive analysis** — assessed landscape, integration challenges with legacy tech systems, cybersecurity concerns
- **Synthesis** — shaped conceptual features around gathered insights
- **UI design** — English-language mockup designed for presentation

## Personal Connection

Leveraged direct knowledge of Turkey to navigate an unfamiliar industry. Bridged an 11-hour time difference and academic schedule to engage with local professionals. Built a network from scratch — initiated conversations with insiders, expanded understanding through introductory meetings.

## Key Themes

- Navigating cultural nuances
- Building new professional relationships
- Synthesizing local knowledge into design decisions
