## What Spiritwood Is

A 2D puzzle-platformer game built in Unity. Players explore an enchanted forest corrupted by a human long ago. As the first new human in centuries, you must work with uncorrupted animals to bring corrupted ones back to the light and reverse the curse.

Core mechanic: **Spirit Mode** — collect spirit wood charges, enter your spirit form to pass through special walls and platforms invisible to your human form. Conserve charges wisely or press R to restart. Designed around "learn by doing" over "learn by reading."

## Design Decisions Worth Highlighting

- **Target audience**: Ages 6+, intentionally female protagonist to encourage female players in a male-dominated platform game genre
- **Types of fun**: Challenge (puzzle-cracking) + Discovery (exploration, NPC dialogue, uncovering your powers)
- **Single-button interaction**: Press E for everything (chat NPCs, collect spirit wood, enter spirit form) — reduces cognitive load so players focus on puzzles
- **Low-stakes restart**: R restarts the level, deliberately making stakes low to encourage experimentation over perfectionism

## Portfolio Framing

Spiritwood is the wildcard project — shows:
- Breadth (narrative + visual + game design, different from app/research work)
- Craft in a completely different medium (pixel art, Unity, game mechanics)
- Design thinking applied to play: cognitive load, UX principles map to game design
- Creative range — enchanted forest world with intentional social message (female protagonist)
